Bridging the Gap Between Education and Industry

Founded in 2005, Taj Technical College was established to solve the shortage of skilled technical talent.

We believe that technical education should be as dynamic as the technology itself. Our campus is more than just a school; it's a high-performance ecosystem where students work on real-world problems using the same tools found in top global laboratories.

Our Journey

2005 - Foundation

TechNova was founded as a small vocational center with a focus on Electrical Engineering and 50 pioneering students.

2012 - Digital Expansion

Launched the Digital Arts and Software Development wing, responding to the global tech boom.

2018 - State-of-the-Art Campus

Inaugurated our current 20-acre tech campus featuring advanced Robotics and Renewable Energy laboratories.

2024 - Industry Integration

Achieved a record 94% placement rate and established dedicated placement offices in 3 major tech hubs.
